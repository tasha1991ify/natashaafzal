Interior Design

BR Restaurant

Spanning 4,800 square feet across two levels, Bin Riaz Grill merges spatial authenticity with modern curation, pairing a warm, tactile material palette with a minimalist vocabulary of refined plasterwork, architectural metal detailing, and terrazzo flooring. The interior is organized into a disciplined three-part longitudinal layout, anchored by a striking central zone featuring an avant-garde hanging wooden installation—an overhanging organic nest meticulously crafted from suspended, angular battens that define the restaurant's signature visual identity.
