Hotel Design

UCHSR - Cappadocia, Turkey

This project involved developing photorealistic visualizations for the UCHSR Hotel, emphasizing the integration of contemporary luxury with Cappadocia's unique geological topography. My workflow focused on material studies and context-sensitive lighting to harmonize architectural forms with the site's historic stone vernacular. Software used: AutoCAD, Sketchup, Lumion, Photoshop.
