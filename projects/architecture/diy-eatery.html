Restaurant Design

DIY Eatery

Grounded in social and environmental sustainability, the proposed interior for the DIY Eatery embraces a cradle-to-cradle design methodology. The material palette highlights repurposed elements, most notably a dynamic overhead gantry constructed from reclaimed kale wood crates. This cascading feature serves a dual purpose as an architectural statement piece and an integrated housing for lighting. Framed against textured white brick walls interspersed with longitudinal timber accents, the space delivers a warm, tactile, and eco-conscious dining environment.
